AI Velocity Cup: F1Tenth Autonomous Racing Challeng

12:30-13:30, on August 21 and August 22, 2024,
co-located with IEEE PACRIM 2024, in David Strong Building.

Timeline:
Registration: Aug. 1 to Aug. 16, 2024
Track setup: Aug. 20, 2024
Inspection time: 9:30-12:00, Aug. 21, 2024
Time Trial: 12:30-13:30, Aug. 21, 2024
Head to head race: 12:30-13:30, Aug. 22, 2024
Award announcement: 14:40, Aug. 22, 2024

Location: David Strong Building, University of Victoria
Map to David Strong Building
Sponsor: IEEE Joint VTS/ComSoc Chapter, Victoria Section
Contact: Lin Cai, cai@uvic.ca
REGISTRATION: This competition is open for everyone of all levels, everyone is welcome to participate in this competition. A team can consist of multiple teammates. Teams with only one person are also allowed. Teams that take part in the in-person competition need to provide and build an F1TENTH car by themselves. To register in the 1st F1tenth Sim Racing League, please refer to the Sim Racing Registration page.
The following Google form is only for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions. Registration to IROS 2024 is expected for all competitors.
